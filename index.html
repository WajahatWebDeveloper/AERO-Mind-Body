Bio

Together, We Can Achieve Wellness!

Dr. Steven Cox, originally from Mississippi, earned his undergraduate degree from Mississippi State University. He then worked for a leading pharmaceutical company, specializing in the central nervous system, cardiovascular medicine, and infectious diseases. Observing concerning trends in the industry, he decided to pursue a doctorate at Logan University's College of Chiropractic. His education encompassed human anatomy, physiology, psychology, biochemistry, nutrition, neurology, biomechanics, physical and lab diagnosis, philosophy, and various conservative natural health techniques. Additionally, Dr. Cox has completed thousands of hours of post-doctoral training in advanced neurology, Traditional Chinese Medicine/Acupuncture, Functional Medicine, Applied Kinesiology, and specialized mind/body techniques. With over 22 years of clinical practice, Dr. Cox has treated a diverse range of patients, addressing conditions from acute muscle and joint injuries to chronic issues such as pain/inflammatory syndromes, hormone imbalances, digestive dysfunction, allergies, fatigue, and stress-related disorders. Outside of his clinical practice, Dr. Cox is a competitive tennis player, participating in adult tournaments and team tennis. He has also practiced various martial arts since a young age and was trained as a cavalry scout by the U.S. Army, serving in a reconnaissance role.
